# STITCH Prompt — Tutivex

## Product Context
Tutivex is an innovative online tutoring platform that connects students with expert educators for personalized, one-on-one learning experiences. It aims to make high-quality, flexible education accessible globally, empowering students to achieve their learning goals and tutors to build thriving teaching practices.

## Design Style
Style: Modern Professional with Engaging Accents
Mood: Trusted, Empowering, and Approachable
Inspiration: Preply.com

## Color Palette

Background: #121117
Surface: #384047
Text: #ffffff
Accent: #99c5ff

## Typography
Heading font: Platform (700 weight)
Body font: Figtree (400 weight)

## Page to Generate: Landing Page

### Layout
The page uses a full-width, vertically stacked section layout. Each section has generous vertical padding (e.g., 96px or 160px). Content within sections is typically centered or arranged in a grid with a maximum width of 1280px.

### Hero Section
Headline: "Master Your Potential. Learn with the Best."
Subheadline: "Connect with expert tutors for personalized, one-on-one learning across any subject. Achieve your goals, on your schedule."
CTA: "Find a Tutor" (Primary button), "Become a Tutor" (Secondary button)
Visual: A large, abstract illustration on the right side, featuring interconnected nodes, subtle glowing elements, and stylized educational motifs (e.g., books, lightbulb) rendered in `#ff7aac` and `#99c5ff` against the `#121117` background. The illustration should convey connection and learning.

### How It Works Section
Headline: "Your Path to Mastery, Simplified."
Subheadline: "Getting started with Tutivex is easy. Here's how."
Layout: Three feature cards arranged horizontally in a grid, each with an icon, title, and description.
Content:
1.  **Find Your Expert**: "Browse profiles, filter by subject, price, and availability. Watch intro videos and read reviews to find your perfect match."
2.  **Book Your Lesson**: "Schedule one-on-one sessions directly through the platform. Secure payments, flexible times."

Visual: Each card features a modern, minimalist icon (e.g., magnifying glass, calendar, video camera) in `#ff7aac` or `#99c5ff`. Cards have a `#384047` background.

### Why Choose Tutivex Section

## Page to Generate: Student Dashboard

### Layout
The page features a two-column layout. A fixed-width left sidebar for navigation and a main content area for dashboard widgets. The main content area uses a flexible grid for its sections.

### Header
Layout: Full-width, sticky at the top.
Content: Tutivex logo (left), "Welcome, Anya!" (center), Profile dropdown (with "My Profile", "Settings", "Logout"), and a "Find Tutors" button (Primary style) on the right.
Visual: Background `#121117`, text `#ffffff`.

### Sidebar Navigation
Layout: Left-aligned, fixed-width column.
Content: List of navigation links with icons: "Dashboard", "My Lessons", "Find Tutors", "Messages", "Billing", "Settings", "Help".
Visual: Background `#121117`. Text `#ffffff`. Active link highlighted with a `#ff7aac` background strip or text color change to `#ff7aac` with a subtle accent. Icons are simple, modern, and white.

### My Lessons Section
Headline: "Upcoming Lessons"
Layout: A list of lesson cards, each representing an upcoming session.
Content: Each card displays the Tutor Name, Subject, Date, Time, and a "Join Lesson" button (Primary style).
Example: "Mark Chen - Advanced Calculus - Mon, Oct 26, 2:00 PM PST"
Visual: Cards have a `#384047` background, `md` border radius, and `sm` box shadow.
